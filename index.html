GRIT

Kevin Yabut

One of the most unique word that I've known in my life is the word Grit. It is a word that suits people who are striving in order to achieve their dreams and goals in life. Grit is passion, courage and perseverance in one word. That's why I decided to start a business in clothing and name it with the word Grit. It's a simple word yet it has a lot of meaning.It represents an individual's ability to strive harder and maintain a focus on long term goals. It is the tenacity to overcome challenges and setbacks, fueled by deep passion for what one aims to achieve. Grit goes beyond innate talent and intelligence, emphasizing the importance of hard work and resilience. The gritty individual understands that success is a marathon, not a sprint and is willing to endure pain and discomfort to reach the finish line. Grit involves sustained effort over time, and its improvement requires a mentality that embraces challenges as a part of journey. That's why this word is really meaningful to me because it kinda summarizes the story of my life. It takes grit to reach my dreams and I'm still on the marathon where the finish line is still far ahead.

  • Passion - a powerful feeling and barely controllable emotion
  • Courage - is the strength in the face of pain and grief
  • Perseverance - persistence in doing something despite the difficulty or delay in achieving success
